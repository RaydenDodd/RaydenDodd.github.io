Tank Game

Top-down tank combat game inspired by classic Wii Tanks–style gameplay. Fight AI enemy tanks with distinct behaviors across multiple levels and a final boss.

JavaScript Game Dev AI
▶ Play Game Repo

Overview

Navigate maze-like levels, battle enemy tanks with different personalities, and adapt to increasing difficulty. Enemies may chase, shoot, drop bombs, or turn invisible depending on their type.

How to Play

Move with WASD, aim with the mouse, and click to shoot. Drop bombs with the space bar. Enemies react dynamically based on distance and line of sight.
